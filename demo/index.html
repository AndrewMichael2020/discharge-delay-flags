How to read this

From thousands of rows to a short shift huddle

  1. Detect: flag stays that cross robust delay thresholds, post-ready caps, or control-chart limits.
  2. Explain: attribute a likely operational delay reason such as transport, home care, diagnostics, or weekend flow.
  3. Compress: group patient-level evidence into a small number of management signals per shift.
  4. Act: produce a CSV-friendly worklist with owner, evidence, and recommended next step.
